Gregory

The Gregory house was built between 1918-1919 due to lack of funds and resources then but the greg house as we all call it, holds lots of memories from our great grand parent to our generations, I was hoping to say generations to come but now that its going out for sale,well i can still say generations to come cos afterall this new family would love it greg was named after great great great gran (Pa Gregory Johnson).

Greg Interior Overview

Greg has a Eight rooms in total excluding the family room, Kitchen, store and the study room,each rooms has their own bathroom there are two family rooms, we have a general one on the ground floor and the mini Fam on the third floor, each floor has four rooms, a lobby leading to two rooms at a time then there is a mini deck on the top most floor thats for relaxation and alone time.The gorund floor has the study room and the other areas.There are three doors leading right ino the gregs house and we have emmergency exit.
